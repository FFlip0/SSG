## Linear Equations

---
$m=\frac{rise}{run}=\frac{y_2-y_1}{x_2-x_1}$

---

Slope-Intercept Form
$y=mx+b$
$y=mx+c$

---
Standard form
$Ax+By=C$

---
Point-Slope form
$y-y_1=m(x-x_1)$


---
Distance
$d= \sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2+(y_2-y_1)^2}$

---
Midpoint
$m=(\frac{x_1+x_2}{2},\frac{y_1+y_2}{2})$

---
Intercept form
$\frac{x}{a}+\frac{y}{b}=1$
$a=x-intercept$
$b=y-intercept$

---
Perpendicular Lines
$m_1\times m_2=-1$
$m_2=-\frac{1}{m_1}$

---
Circle
$(x-a)^2+(y-b)^2=r^2$
$(a,b) =$ Centre point of the circle
$r =$ Radius

## Quadratics
### Non-Monic Factorisation 
Find $x$
$x^2+3x+2=0$
---
$a+b=3$ 
$a\times b=2$
$a=2$
$b=1$

$(x+2)(x+1)=0$

| $x+2=0$ | $x+1=0$ |
| ------- | ------- |
| $x=-2$  | $x=-1$  |
$x=-2,-1$

### Monic Factorisation
Find $x$
$2x^2+3x+1=0$
---
$a+b=3$
$a\times b=1\times 2$
$a=1$ 
$b=2$

$(x+1)(x+2)=0$

| $x+1=0$ | $x+2=0$ |
| ------- | ------- |
| $x=-1$  | $x=-2$  |
$x=-1,-2$
