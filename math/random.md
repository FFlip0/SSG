## Quadratics
### Non-Monic Factorisation 
Find $x$
$x^2+3x+2=0$
---
$a+b=3$ 
$a\times b=2$
$a=2$
$b=1$

$(x+2)(x+1)=0$

| $x+2=0$ | $x+1=0$ |
| ------- | ------- |
| $x=-2$  | $x=-1$  |
$x=-2,-1$

### Monic Factorisation
Find $x$
$2x^2+3x+1=0$
---
$a+b=3$
$a\times b=1\times 2$
$a=1$ 
$b=2$

$(x+1)(x+2)=0$

| $x+1=0$ | $x+2=0$ |
| ------- | ------- |
| $x=-1$  | $x=-2$  |
$x=-1,-2$
